Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains what data MoneyMooring ("we", "us") collects when you visit moneymooring.com and how it is used.

Data we collect

  • Usage data: pages viewed, referring URLs, approximate location (country/region), device and browser type — collected via analytics tools using cookies or similar technologies.
  • Advertising data: our advertising partners, including Google and search feed providers, may use cookies and identifiers to measure ad performance and show relevant sponsored content. These partners process data under their own privacy policies.
  • Contact data: if you email us, we retain your message and address to respond.

How we use data

To operate and improve the site, measure content performance, display and measure advertising, and respond to inquiries. We do not sell personal information as the term is commonly understood; advertising partners may process identifiers for ad delivery and measurement as described above.

Cookies and choices

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Where required by law, a consent banner lets you accept or decline non-essential cookies. Opting out of interest-based advertising is also available via industry tools such as the NAI and DAA opt-out pages and, for Google, via Google Ads Settings.

Third-party services

The site may use Google AdSense / AdSense for Search and web analytics services. Google's use of advertising cookies is described in Google's advertising privacy documentation.

Data retention and security

Analytics data is retained per the default retention settings of our analytics providers. Email correspondence is kept as long as needed to handle the inquiry. We apply reasonable technical measures to protect data but no internet transmission is fully secure.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including the EU/EEA, UK and certain US states), you may have rights to access, correct or delete personal data, or object to processing. Contact editor@moneymooring.com to exercise them.

Children

The site is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their data.

Changes

We may update this policy; the date above reflects the latest revision.

MoneyMooring

MoneyMooring is an independent publisher of personal finance information. We are not a bank, lender, insurer or financial advisor. Content is educational and should not be taken as individual financial advice.
